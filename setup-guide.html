Before You Start
What You Need
This guide walks you through setting up your cold email automation app from scratch. You will not use the terminal or command line at all. Everything is done through websites.
You need three things before starting:
1
A Google account (Gmail works) — used for Neon and Vercel
2
A GitHub account — your repos are already created:
• Frontend: github.com/interiorvisualsd-maker/cold-outreach-frontend
• Backend: github.com/interiorvisualsd-maker/cold-outreach-backend
3
20–30 minutes of focused time
💡 How it works: You're setting up three free websites that work together. Neon stores your data, Render runs the backend (sends emails), and Vercel hosts the website you log into.
✅ Why Render instead of Google Cloud? Render's free tier doesn't require a credit card, has no fraud-prevention blocks, and works in every country. Google Cloud's billing verification can be difficult to pass in some regions.
Step 1 · 5 minutes
Create Your Database (Neon)
Neon stores all your data — leads, emails, replies, and accounts
1
Open https://neon.tech in your browser
2
Click "Sign up" (top right corner)
3
Click "Continue with Google" and pick your Google account
4
On the "Create your first project" page:
• Project name: type lead-dispatcher
• Database name: leave as neondb
• Region: pick the closest to you (if unsure, pick US East (Ohio))
• Postgres version: leave default
5
Click "Create project"
6
You'll see a page with a long connection string that looks like:
postgresql://lead_dispatcher_owner:AbCdEf@ep-cool-name.us-east-2.aws.neon.tech/neondb?sslmode=require
⚠️ IMPORTANT: Copy this ENTIRE link and save it somewhere safe (Notes app, password manager, etc.). You'll need it 2 more times. This is your DATABASE LINK.
Step 2 · 3 minutes
Generate Your Secret Keys
Your app needs 3 secret keys to keep passwords and data safe
Key 1: CRON SECRET (for the email worker)
1
Go to https://www.random.org/strings
2
Set: Length = 32, Strings = 1, Character set = Alphanumeric
3
Click "Get Strings" → copy the result
Key 2: JWT SECRET (for user passwords)
1
Go to https://www.allkeysgenerator.com/Random/Security-Encryption-Key-Generator.aspx
2
Set key size to 256-bit and click "Generate"
3
Copy the hex string
Key 3: ENCRYPTION KEY (for email account passwords)
1
On the same page, click "Generate" again
2
Copy the new hex string
⚠️ Save all 3 keys somewhere safe. You'll need them in the next step.

Scroll down to the "Environment" section. Click "Add Environment Variable" for each of these 6 variables:

3D. Copy your Backend URL

https://cold-outreach-api-abc123.onrender.com

💡 Free tier note: Render's free tier "spins down" your service after 15 minutes of inactivity. The first request after spin-down takes ~30 seconds to wake up. This is normal and won't affect email sending (the cron job keeps it active).
Step 4 · 3 minutes
Set Up the Email Worker
Use cron-job.org to trigger email sending every 5 minutes — free, no signup
Your app needs to check every 5 minutes: "Are there emails to send? Any new replies?" We use cron-job.org for this — it's free and doesn't require an account.
1
Open https://cron-job.org in your browser
2
Click "Sign Up" (top right) — quick email + password signup
3
After logging in, click "Create Cronjob"
4
Fill in:
• Title: Lead Dispatcher Worker
• URL: type your BACKEND URL + /api/cron/ + your CRON SECRET
  Example: https://cold-outreach-api-abc123.onrender.com/api/cron/AbCdEf123456
  (Replace with your actual BACKEND URL and CRON SECRET)
• Execution Schedule: select "Every 5 minutes"
• Request Method: POST
5
Click "Create"
✅ That's it! cron-job.org will now ping your backend every 5 minutes. Your app will automatically send queued emails, check for replies, run warm-up, and break sequences when leads reply.

Troubleshooting
Common Issues & Fixes
🔧 "Failed to fetch" or blank page
Your website can't reach your backend. Check:
1. In Vercel: is NEXT_PUBLIC_API_URL set to your Render BACKEND URL?
2. In Render: is FRONTEND_URL set to your Vercel URL?
3. Did Render finish deploying (status = "Live")?
🔧 "Unauthorized" errors
Your login expired. Just log out and log back in.
🔧 Database won't connect
Your Neon link needs ?sslmode=require at the end:
postgresql://user:pass@host/db?sslmode=require
🔧 Render service is slow / "spun down"
Free tier services sleep after 15 min of inactivity. The first request takes ~30 seconds to wake up. The cron-job.org worker (Step 4) keeps it active by pinging every 5 minutes.
🔧 Email sending fails
1. Gmail: You MUST use an App Password, not your regular password
2. Daily cap too high: Start with 50, not 100
3. Account needs warm-up: New accounts should warm up for 1–2 weeks
🔧 Render build fails
1. Make sure the Dockerfile is in the root of the backend repo
2. Make sure the branch is main
3. Check the build logs in Render for specific errors
